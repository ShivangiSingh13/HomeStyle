Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Name and email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Product preferences and feedback

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Sending shipping and transaction notifications
  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our products and services
  • Sending promotional emails (with your consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information, including SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, and secure server infrastructure. However, no online transmission is completely secure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These help us understand how you use our site and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted partners for order fulfillment and shipping purposes only, under confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or by posting the updated policy on our website.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

Contact us at privacy@homestyle.com or visit our contact page.
