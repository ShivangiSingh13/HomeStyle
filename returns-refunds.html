Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free 30-day return policy with full money-back guarantee

Our 30-Day Return Guarantee

At HomeStyle, we're confident you'll love your purchase. If you're not satisfied for any reason, return your item within 30 days for a full refund.

✓ Return Requirements

  • • Item must be in unused and resaleable condition
  • • Original packaging must be intact and in good condition
  • • All original tags and labels must be attached
  • • Return must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • • All assembly materials and accessories must be included

📦 How to Return an Item

1

Contact Support

Email us or call our team to initiate a return

2

Get Return Label

We'll provide you with a prepaid shipping label

3

Ship the Item

Pack securely and ship using the provided label

4

Receive Refund

Refund processed within 7-10 business days

❌ Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • • Clearance or final sale items
  • • Customized or personalized items
  • • Items used or damaged by customer
  • • Items purchased over 30 days ago

💰 Refund Information

  • • Refunds are issued to your original payment method
  • • Processing time: 7-10 business days after receiving item
  • • Bank transfers may take additional 2-3 business days
  • • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item is defective

Defective Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, please contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.
