Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our furniture, shipping, returns, and customer support.

Common Questions

+ Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over ₹4,000. For orders under ₹4,000, standard shipping is available at a minimal cost. Express shipping options are also available for faster delivery.

+ What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

+ How long does delivery take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number to monitor delivery progress.

+ Are your products eco-friendly?

Many of our products are made from sustainable and eco-friendly materials. We're committed to reducing our environmental impact. Check individual product descriptions for material details.

+ Do you offer customization?

Some products can be customized based on color, size, or material. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific requirements and see what options are available.

+ Is assembly included?

Most furniture items come with detailed assembly instructions and necessary tools. We also offer professional assembly services in select locations. Contact us for more information.

+ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital payment systems, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with SSL encryption.

+ Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. After that, your order will have been processed for shipment. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.
