Shipping Information

Fast and reliable delivery options to get your furniture to your doorstep safely

Shipping Options

🚚 Standard Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 5-7 business days
  • Cost: FREE for orders over ₹4,000 | ₹799 for orders under ₹4,000
  • Best For: Budget-conscious customers

⚡ Express Shipping

  • Delivery Time: 2-3 business days
  • Cost: ₹1,599 (any order size)
  • Best For: Urgent orders

Shipping Policy

✓ Order Processing

All orders are processed within 24 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email with an order number and tracking details.

✓ Packaging & Safety

We use premium packaging materials to ensure your furniture arrives in perfect condition. All items are carefully wrapped and protected.

✓ Tracking & Notifications

You'll receive email updates at each stage of your delivery. Track your order in real-time using your tracking number.

✓ Delivery Signature

Large furniture items may require a signature upon delivery for security purposes. Our carriers will contact you with delivery time windows.

✓ Local Pickup

For customers in the New Delhi area, local pickup is available. Contact us to arrange a pickup time at our showroom.
